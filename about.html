Hi! I’m Aravind S, an electronics engineer passionate about creating innovative solutions that enhance everyday life. I hold a Master of Science Degree in Electronics with specialisation in VLSI and AI from Digital University Kerala and have been fascinated by technology since childhood. I built my first circuit at the age of 12. Most of my learning comes from practical experience. I’ve completed several home projects, including computer numerical controls, a humanoid robot, and multiple utility devices, which allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios. My curriculum projects further enhanced these skills. My journey has involved designing embedded systems, power supply units, and application-specific ICs. I specialise in analogue circuits, embedded systems, and research, and I'm proficient in multiple CAD tools, EDA tools, FPGA tools, and Linux environments.

My strong problem-solving abilities help me tackle complex challenges in engineering. I’m proud to have received the Best Lab Performer in PG and participated in the state-level Young Innovators award conducted by KDISC Govt. of Kerala. I am a Rashtrapathi Scout with Bharat Scouts and Guides and an A Certificate holder in the National Cadet Corps. I have also volunteered with the National Service Scheme during both my higher secondary and degree studies. I am actively seeking opportunities to volunteer in socially impactful activities. I believe in technology for humanity and strive to incorporate sustainability into my engineering practices. When I’m not working, I enjoy experiencing new things, watching sci-fi movies, and volunteering in social activities. Feel free to reach out at my email or connect with me on LinkedIn. I’m excited about the future of technology and look forward to creating impactful solutions!
